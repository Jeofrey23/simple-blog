About Me

All The Time,All Content

Hi, I’m Jeofrey Bandisa, and welcome to my blog! I’m a passionate traveler with a particular love for bridges. Over the years, I’ve explored countless iconic spans that not only offer stunning views but also tell the stories of the places they connect. From ancient stone structures to modern engineering feats, I’m fascinated by how bridges bridge both literal and cultural divides. I started this blog as a way to share my experiences, photos, and the fascinating history behind each bridge I visit. My goal is to inspire others to travel, explore, and appreciate these incredible structures that often go unnoticed. Whether you’re a fellow bridge enthusiast, a curious traveler, or someone simply looking for travel tips, I hope you’ll find something that sparks your interest here.

When I’m not on the road, you can usually find me researching my next adventure, learning more about the history of bridges, or trying to capture that perfect photograph of a bridge at sunset. Join me on my journey as I share stories, tips, and a deeper look at the bridges that shape our world.
