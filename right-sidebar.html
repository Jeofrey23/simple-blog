Lub-ban Bridge

When I first heard about the Lubban, Nansubli,Petwa-guey bridges I knew it was a place I had to visit. Located in the heart of Camanpaguey Cabitin, this bridge is more than just a means of crossing; it’s a living piece of history and a marvel of engineering. From the moment I arrived, I could feel the weight of the stories embedded in its structure, and as I walked across, I was transported to another time. Here's a glimpse into my experience at the Lub-ban Bridge.

Arriving at the Lub-ban Bridge

I started my journey by public transport. As I approached the bridge, I was immediately struck by its towering presence and the way it seemed to perfectly blend with the surrounding landscape. The river or valley beneath the bridge was calm and reflective, creating a picturesque scene that made me pause to take in the beauty. The first thing that struck me was the scale of the bridge. Standing beneath it, I took a few moments to admire the intricate details of its design—the on how residents able to perform the works.
